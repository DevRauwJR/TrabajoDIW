¿WHO WE ARE?

Welcome to Oil Jaén: Pioneers in Oil Excellence


OOil Jaén is more than a company, we are a tradition rooted in the sunny land of Jaén, Spain. We pride ourselves on being experts in producing the highest quality olive oil, capturing the authentic flavour and aroma of the region.

Located in the heart of Jaén, our company has cultivated and perfected the art of producing olive oil of excellence. Our passion for tradition and quality is reflected in every drop of our precious oil,
from the moment the olives are carefully harvested to the creation of the final product.
