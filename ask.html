ASK



What is the quality of your oils?

At Oil Jaén we are proud to offer olive oils of the highest quality, made with carefully selected olives and production processes that follow rigorous standards of excellence.




Where do your olives come from?

Our olives come from local olive groves in the Jaén region of Spain. We work closely with local farmers who share our passion for quality and sustainability.




How can I place an order?

You can place an order through our online shop. Select the products you want, add them to the cart and follow the secure checkout process.




What is the difference between the different types of olive oil?

There are different types of olive oil, such as extra virgin, virgin and refined. The main difference lies in the quality and the extraction process. Our extra virgin oils maintain the purity and natural flavour of the olives.




How do I store olive oil correctly?

To maintain freshness and quality, store olive oil in a cool, dark place, away from direct light and heat. Be sure to close the bottle tightly after each use.




Do you ship internationally?

yes, we ship internationally. You can check shipping availability to your location during the checkout process in our online shop.




Do you have gift options available?

Yes, we offer gift options. You can select the gift option during the checkout process and add a personalised message if you wish.
